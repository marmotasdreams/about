Marmotas
Dreams

Marmotas dreams is a project developed in the dialogue between experimental music and visual art produced with code. A poetic experience, a joint hunt into the heart and meaning of things and the language. We experiment with the significant structures of music, sound art, computer language and the vocal and phonetic performance of poetic texts in different languages. We compose works in feedback processes between sound art, poetry, experimental and electronic music, performance, improvisation, installation and live programming.
